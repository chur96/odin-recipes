Chicken

Description

Crispy chicken thighs ideal for meal prepping as a week's worth of food. Serve with rice and veggies

Ingredients

  • 8 - 9 lbs of chicken thighs without skin and bone
  • Spices of choice

Steps

  1. Pressure cook chicken in 3 lb. batches with 1.5 cups of water 10 minutes
  2. Cut chicken into smaller bite sizes
  3. Air fry for 12 - 15 minutes with spices, shaking basket occassionally
